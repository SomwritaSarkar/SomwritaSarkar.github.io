I am an Urban Science researcher studying spatial and socio-economic inequalities in cities. My work informs urban planning and policy by employing methods from spatial data science and modeling, geography, economics, physics, and complex systems science.

I lead the Urban Science Lab and am a member of the Transport Lab, Urban Housing Lab, Smart Urbanism Lab, and the Centre for Complex Systems at the University of Sydney. I serve on the Editorial Board of Environment and Planning B: Urban Analytics and City Science. I serve as the Editor for Urban Findings.
